Services and Solutions

At CSC India, with our in-depth knowledge in various technology areas, we serve our clients with complete accountability. Because we understand the imperatives of our clients’ businesses, we have developed and adopted robust processes to ensure that we deliver quality consistently. Our domain experience and technological expertise results in innumerable tangible benefits for our clients.

Our unique value proposition lies in our ability to:

  • Use our global delivery model for providing top quality services
  • Allow clients to benefit from over half a decade of rich IT services experience and expertise
  • Leverage our top quality processes and infrastructure capabilities (SEI-CMM 5, CMMi 3, PCMM 5, ISO/IEC 27001:2005, BS15000 certified)
  • Focus on re-usability thus reducing cycle times and costs
  • Leverage our top-notch talent pool, over 40% of who are internationally certified domain experts
  • Innovate continuously and stay at the forefront of technology and management development
