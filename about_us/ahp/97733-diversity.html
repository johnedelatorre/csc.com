  • Overview

    • Overview

      At CSC, we view diversity beyond the race, gender, age, sexual orientation, gender identity, abilities and ethnicity of the person.

      Diversity is reflected in our inclusive environment that embraces the many cultures, backgrounds, values and ideas offered by our global workforce, who collectively drive and influence individual and organizational success.

  • Our Programs

    • Our Programs

      CSC's programs are designed to provide employees with opportunities to engage with leaders and colleagues around the globe to make a difference for our company, clients and communities.

      There are many ways to contribute, including creating innovative business solutions, taking on leadership roles in support of our diversity and inclusion strategy, and getting involved with outreach efforts in communities where we work and live.


      Employee Resource Groups

      Employee Resource Groups (ERGs) are another way CSC fosters a diverse workplace. These groups are created by employees, for employees

      Supplier Diversity

      An award-winning program, the supplier-diversity program awards more than $1 billion a year in contracts to women- and minority-owned businesses, small businesses and other diverse suppliers
