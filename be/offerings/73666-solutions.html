Solutions

At CSC, we have the scale and expertise to create a complete solution for your organisation. From front-end consulting and planning, to integrating and even managing your technology solutions, we have the depth and experience to respond to your unique challenges and opportunities. Explore the key solutions we deliver in Belgium:

 

SAP Solutions & Services
CSC offers a wide range of SAP solutions and services defined to meet a variety of client needs, spanning size, scope and SAP footprints.

 

Cloud Computing
A vendor-independent IT services delivery model that puts your business and security first in order to offer a trustable cloud solution that leads to business agility, helps lower operating costs, and removes IT barriers to innovation.

Customer Relationship Management
Business consulting and advanced technologies to help you identify and manage customer segments, foster customer-satisfying behavior, and implement customer-centric strategies, along with supporting processes and tools.

Information Exploitation
Access to detailed, accurate data about your company’s real performance and the forces affecting your business to help your people make the right operational and strategic decisions that lead to optimized and improved corporate performance.

Mobile Solutions
The opening of the mobile channel via specific mobile services to allow your business to respond more rapidly to new and changing business requirements, and manage conflicting demands more effectively.

Organizational Change Management
The methodology and experience, the tools and the experts that make sure your organization stays firmly on course during times of upheaval and change. Together, we take your business toward a successful future.

Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) Excellence
Our Software Development Life-Cycle solution (SDLC) offers a robust yet lean and pragmatic approach to optimizing your IT resources.
