Health Services Insights

The Global Institute for Emerging Healthcare Practices is the applied research arm of CSC's Healthcare Group. The mission of the Institute is to track worldwide trends, conduct multi-country studies and evaluate emerging operational practices and technologies that have the potential to improve performance of health industries around the world. By merging trends and experience across geographies, we help healthcare providers learn about and capitalize on best practices no matter where they are developed.

Featured white papers:

Coordinated Care: Assembling Patient Information that Matters: Although the current investment in EHRs is a critical building block, organizations that put more comprehensive patient information to work will have better results delivering coordinated care under value-based insurance designs like accountable care.

 

Preventing Hospital Readmissions: The First Test Case for Continuity of Care: The next phase of work to minimize hospital readmissions will achieve a formal connection with organized care management for every patient covered by this type of program.


TeleServices for Better Health: The Decade of the Non-Doctor Clinician: The growing use of technology in the healthcare workforce will change how people work, what's expected of them, and what types of skills will be most valued.

TeleServices for Better Health: Technology that Brings Healthcare to the Patient: TeleServices bring key care activities directly to patients and empower them to become more engaged in their own care. To deploy TeleServices successfully, organizations should consider the latest technologies and design ideas.
