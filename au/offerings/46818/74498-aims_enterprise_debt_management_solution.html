Debt Management Solution - AIMS

Revolutionising Debt Management with AIMS

AIMS is CSC’s debt management solution for financial institutions, agencies and any other organisation with a requirement for debt collection.

Improving your bottom line by reducing bad debt

Debt recovery is becoming an increasingly important part of many organisations' strategies to reduce capital exposure, particularly in light of the global financial crisis.

The average company has at least 40 per cent of its assets tied up in the debtor's ledger , with collection processes and debt management consuming considerable time, money and resources.

The AIMS all-in-one debt management solution provides organisations with an automated, intuitive platform that offers substantial benefits to shareholders, employees and customers.

Whether your business operates in banking, utilities or any number of other sectors, our software can facilitate company-wide integration, improve regulatory compliance and enhance customer satisfaction.

Automating and innovating debt collection

Making collections can be a time-consuming and cumbersome task, which can often be made worse by inefficient processes and complicated procedures.

One of the key problems companies face is that customer debts can be spread across a variety of departments and account names.

AIMS' innovative automating features mean that your collectors can maximise their effectiveness by efficiently monitoring multiple debt profiles.

Rather than having disparate accounts overseen by a variety of different departments, our software brings together all of a customer's arrears onto one screen. This means whether a customer owes money on credit cards, insurance or a mortgage, all debt information can be accessed by one collector through a single-screen user interface. Not only does this optimise call ratios by allowing collectors to cover numerous issues with a single call, it prevents customers from receiving multiple calls in a day - which can result in frustration, broken promises to pay and businesses transgressing industry regulations.

Customisation not configuration

The financial services industry is a fast-paced and ever-changing environment, with the most successful businesses being those that are prepared to evolve quickly.

Our highly-customisable software means businesses of all sizes, from small mercantile agent operations to large multinational banks, can implement the solution with a minimum of fuss.

Whether your organisation wants a straight out of the box deployment or requires a complex integration across multiple platforms, AIMS puts the power in your hands through an innovative rules system.

While other software providers may rely on consultants or extensive configuration during the implementation stage, our product enables you to set initial parameters for your operations during implementation, however also provides you with the flexibility to self-adjust the parameters as frequently as required to suit your business needs. The rules system includes scope for testing to ensure that your parameter changes will result in the desired outcome for your business.

This boosts company agility and flexibility, allowing your enterprise to cope with fluctuating business conditions such as natural disasters and other abnormal processing scenarios - a characteristic rarely found in other software or in-house applications.

Financial benefits

Since the global financial crisis, many organisations have faced a challenging operating environment, making efficiency gains and cost reduction a top priority.

The financial benefits of an AIMS implementation are wide-ranging, encompassing reduced exposure to bad debt, streamlining of departments and staff, and may lead to share price increases.

Using an automated queuing system, collectors are able to efficiently progress through their daily workload, reducing backlogs and delays, while benefiting from an intuitive interface that prioritises customers.

Our online repayment calculator provides your collector with fast real-time decision-making ability, with flexible payment options ensuring every customer is offered an affordable payment plan while on the phone. This prevents payment promises being broken, while increasing debts collected, raising the number of accounts worked over a given period and improving your team's key performance indicator achievements.

On a company-wide scale, by boosting debts recovered your organisation can reduce capital provisions for exposure to bad arrears, resulting in increased share prices and more dividends.

Employee benefits

Employees working in debt collection often have to cope with various issues that make their role more difficult, including irritable customers, a multitude of technology systems and the stress that comes with meeting daily, weekly and monthly targets.

AIMS' intuitive all-in-one interface helps solve all of these problems and possesses a number of other advantages for your collectors, making them feel more empowered and motivated.

By simplifying processes and reducing the amount of administration work, our software enables your collection staff to manage a broader workload with less specialist training.

Integrated call recording functions also allow team leaders to more adequately focus their training exercises, boosting the quality of coaching and raising skill levels while reducing staff turnover.

Our Visual Rules Engine enables quick and easy modification of work streams, which facilitates rapid response to a number of unforeseen events such as corporate collapses.

Customer benefits

Relationship banking has become a central focus for many financial organisations, with the sector's competitive environment encouraging a customer-centric approach.

AIMS utilises multi-channel communications - including email, letters and SMS - as well as web services to ensure account balances are kept up-to-date in light of real-time banking.

This means your customers can benefit from a bespoke service depending on their specific circumstances, while ensuring they do not receive multiple or excessive payment requests.

The platform's customisation features can also ensure collectors remain sensitive when dealing with customers affected by external mitigating factors, including natural disasters.

These customer-centric features ensure a smoother debt collection experience for your customers, which greatly increases the likelihood of a higher overall recovery rate for your organisation.
