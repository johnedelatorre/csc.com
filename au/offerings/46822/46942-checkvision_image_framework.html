CheckVision Image Framework

CSC’s CheckVision® Image Framework is a high-performance, transaction-delivery infrastructure. It delivers archived customer reports, statements, check and lockbox images and associated payment data to customers. The results are delivered by mail, CD-ROM/DVD or the Internet.The proven architecture enables new delivery services for retail and corporate customers while reducing operating expenses and fraud exposure.

Comprehensive cash management services in CheckVision Image Framework include image ARP, lockbox and controlled disbursement. CheckVision Image Framework’s common interface for systems used in the delivery and viewing of check images allows a bank’s customers to mix and match delivery types as desired. This service saves banks money by eliminating the preparation and shipment of large volumes of paper. It also has the potential to generate fee income.

CheckVision Image Framework can stand alone or be integrated with CheckVision archive or with third-party industry archive solutions. Adding CheckVision Image Delivery applications allows banks to offer their retail and corporate cash management customers a range of image delivery choices that include paper, CD/DVD and via the Internet. These options include:

  • CheckVision Image Statements
  • CheckVision e-Data Delivery, which includes:
    • CheckVision CD-ROM Plus
    • CheckVision Lockbox CD-ROM
    • CheckVision Virtual CD-ROM
    • CheckVision Encryption
    • CheckVision Scheduler

Key Features

  • High-volume transaction delivery solution
  • Lower operating expenses
  • Reduced fraud exposure
  • Fee-based services for retail and corporate customers
  • Improved customer service, trust and goodwill
  • Replacement for IRD returns and printed reports
  • Integrated daily cash management feeds possible
