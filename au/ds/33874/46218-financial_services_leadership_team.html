Financial Services Leadership Team

Meet CSC's Financial Services executives who are guiding and executing our vision and strategy.

Stephen Kowal is the General Manager for financial services for CSC Australia, and is accountable for CSC financial services business in Australia and New Zealand.
Stephen has an extensive background in the commercial and IT environment, including prior roles as CIO of BHP Billiton Base Metals based in South America, CIO WMC and BHP Billiton IT Integration Lead in which he led the technical integration of WMC into BHP Billiton as part of the $9.2B acquisition.

He has been involved in numerous acquisitions and divestments, large projects in many industries from Financial Services through to Natural Resources. Earlier in this career he held roles within telecommunications, construction and defence industries. Stephen has significant global experience having worked in, and lived in, North and South America and having run complex projects and lead teams in Australia, Europe, South Africa, North America and South America.

Stephen is a Fellow of the Financial Services Institute of Australasia (FFIN), a Senior Member of the Australian Computer Society (MACS) and a Member of the Australian Institute of Company Directors (MAICD).

::

Simon Millett is Director of Banking at CSC Australia. His role is to spearhead CSC's banking strategy in Australia. To achieve this positions the company as a more significant enterprise-wide provider of technology, systems integration, consulting and software and systems development to the Australian banks.

Simon has 23 years experience in banking and finance and has built a deep understanding of the industry through senior executive assignments in Australia, UK, the Pacific, Asia and Africa, including four MD / CEO roles in subsidiaries of Westpac Banking Corporation and Standard Chartered Bank. Most recently Simon was Standard Chartered's Group Head, Service and Operational Excellence, based in Singapore.

Cecil Roets is Head of Banking Operations for CSC Australia and is responsible for the operational performance of key banking accounts and for the acquisition and delivery of client project outcomes, particularly Hogan opportunity, transition and implementation programs.

Cecil joined CSC in 2001 as Account Executive responsible for Banking Accounts in South Africa. Prior to this, he had 30 years in various Senior Management positions within Standard Bank. This included Managing Computer Operations, Project Director of major projects such as the roll-out of the Bank's Branch Delivery System and Managing of a large Application Development team.

As part of Cecil's Account Executive responsibilities with CSC South Africa he headed up CSC's Hogan Centre of Excellence. The centre provides full Systems Development Life Cycle for it's Core Banking and Card systems in the form of CSC's offering of Managed Services.

Stephen Browne is Director of Insurance for CSC Australia. Previously he held the position of Vice President Asia Pacific for Sirius Financial Systems since February 2004. Sirius was later acquired by SSP where Stephen assumed the same role. Before joining Sirius he was Business Development Director for Lumley Technology (part of the Lumley Insurance group). Stephen has more than 30 years experience in information technology, constructing and delivering innovative, creative e-business solutions backed by many years developing and project managing large corporate mainframe applications.

Stephen spent over 6 years growing and establishing business in very competitive markets in the USA. Subsequently he built and managed a team of over 200 information technology consultants. Later he became Vice President for the group jointly responsible for over 2000 information technology professionals.

He has delivered in industries such as insurance, mining, airlines, retail, education, and government. Stephen specifically sold and delivered solutions to clients including QBE, IAG, Lumley Insurance and NSW office of State revenue.

David Welsh has just been appointed Head of Life Insurance for CSC Australia. David has more than 25 years experience in information technology and Life Insurance. He has consulted and implemented systems across the UK, Europe and New Zealand and for the last 10 years has been based in Australia.

He has a wide range of experience, including consulting, constructing and delivering innovative, creative business solutions to meet customer requirements, as well as project, software and people management.

Responsibilities include accountability for the delivery of all financial services applications business within Australia and New Zealand. This covers management of local Insurance products, performed Account Management roles and led successful New Business pursuit teams.

Prior to CSC, David worked in the UK building custom IT solutions across a range of industries, including defence, commercial television and financial services (customers included Scottish Widows, Lloyds TSB group & Perpetual).
Justin Begg is Head of Strategy for financial services for CSC Australia. A highly creative and strategic minded business leader, with international experience with organisations such as National Australia Bank, GE, RACV, Medibank, Colonial and County Natwest.

He has a strong ability to define corporate strategies and objectives to challenge and focus organisations, launch new products and run M&A / alliance activities.
Very customer and results-driven with a strong commercial focus, whilst also having the ability to influence and passionately lead people in a way that engenders trust and loyalty. 25 years experience in skillfully engaging and managing diverse stakeholders, particularly within Financial Services.
