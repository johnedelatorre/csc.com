Welcome, and a bioinformatics white paper

2010-02-09


Welcome!

Hello, and welcome to the first of a new informational message series from DynPort Vaccine Company LLC (DVC), a CSC company.

We’ve been working in biopharmaceutical product development as a government contractor for 13 years now, and have learned a few things along the way.  Our goal is to share this information with our partners and potential partners, who may be interested in developments related to government contracting and biopharmaceuticals.

For example, we’ll share information and events related to:

  • Working with the government, and what it takes to succeed
  • Biopharmaceutical product development
  • Various related technical and business topics (for example, Animal Rule, bioinformatics and risk management)

To subscribe to these updates by email, sign up here.

 

Special Feature

We’re pleased to offer you this special edition white paper from DVC President Dr. Robert V. House: “Bioinformatics and the Future of Medical Research and Clinical Practice.”

The enormous advances in biological technology over the past four decades have led to a profound change in how information is processed. Conceptual and technical developments in disciplines such as genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, metabolomics, immunomics, and countless other "omics" have resulted in a veritable sea of data with the potential to radically alter biomedicine. The discipline of bioinformatics has emerged to capture the information stored in living systems and help turn it into actionable technology.  In this paper, the discipline, the tools, and the potential for the future are explored.

Download your copy of this paper here. 

And if you haven't already, check out Dr. House's Bugs and Drugs blog for more news and views on biopharmaceutical product development.
