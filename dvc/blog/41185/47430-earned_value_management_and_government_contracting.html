Earned Value Management and Government Contracting

2010-06-03

Welcome!

On behalf of all of us at DynPort Vaccine Company LLC (DVC), a CSC company, thanks for taking time to read our updates.  We'd love to hear from you, so if there is something you would like to see in this newsletter, please let us know.

Special Feature

Earned value management integrates scope, cost and schedule into a methodology for maintaining a budget with any project.  Since 1997 DVC has maintained a
validated Earned Value Management System (EVMS) from our work on a prime systems contract.  Government agencies increasingly require a validated EVMS system for financial reporting.  This presentation, prepared by DVC's Earned
Value Manager, Ida Davis--one of DVC's certified Earned Value Professionals--provides an overview of EVM for government contracting.

Download your complimentary copy of "Government Contracting and Earned Value Management" here.

Upcoming Events – Join Us!

June 10, 2010, Rockville, MD – The Tech Council of Maryland MdBio division will host Creating Value through Outsourcing, part of its popular BioBreakfast series.  DVC is a BioBreakfast series sponsor, and several DVC representatives will be attending this event.

June 14-17, 2010, Washington, DC – DVC is a Silver Sponsor of the 8th Annual Biodefense Vaccines & Therapeutics conference, hosted by Infocast.  DVC representatives will be discussing animal models and working with the DoD, and New Business representatives will be available to discuss potential partnering opportunities.

June 17-18, 2010, Washington, DC – Thomson Reuters Business will be offering a FAR Workshop on how to understand and use the Federal Acquisition Regulation in your daily contracting activities. (Additional dates and locations available.)

June 23, 2010, Rockville, MD – Join the Tech Council of Maryland’s MdTech division for its Government Contracting Academy, where attendees will learn the tools necessary to be successful in the government contracting industry. Topics include contracting issues, procurement processes and business processes.

June 24, 2010, North Bethesda, MD – The Tech Council of Maryland’s MdTech division will host a Government Contractor Summer Networking Reception. Join others from the Mid-Atlantic government contracting community to meet, network and connect or find your next partnering opportunity.

July 19-20, 2010, Arlington, VA – Join Thomson Reuters Business as they host The Government Contract Negotiations Workshop. This course will cover specifics of negotiating contract and subcontract provisions, prices and claims such as maintaining control and negotiating each key element.
