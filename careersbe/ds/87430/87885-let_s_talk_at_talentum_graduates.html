Let's Talk at Talentum - Graduates

Looking for a new challenge? Come and meet CSC at the bi-annual Talentum job fair organized by jobsite and recruitment magazine Vacature/Références.


Not only can you discover more about our company, services, solutions, and culture, but you can immediately find out if we have an opportunity that matches your profile. And you can meet our recruiters, human resources specialists, and managers, as well as your potential new colleagues. CSC consultants will be on hand to talk to you about what CSC offers professionals at all levels of the organization.
