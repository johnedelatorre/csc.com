  • Managed Security Services

    • Managed Security Services

      CYBERCONFIDENCE for Global Organizations

      Managed Security Services

      Extend the security of your business by enabling extensive visibility, increased compliance, rapid response and the ability to manage threats.

      CSC Enhance

      CSC Enhance is a package of core Cybersecurity services to provide cost-effective protection of assets, while meeting regulatory compliance requirements, and providing incident and vulnerability management reporting.

      CSC Extend

      CSC Extend provides clients with a package of security solutions that have been integrated to an enterprise security dashboard that provides clients visibility, real-time monitoring and improved security decision making.

      CSC Elevate

      CSC Elevate provides clients with integrated services based on the concept that the threat landscape is fluid, and the need for actionable intelligence, constant analysis and correlation of events and their potential impact to the environment.

      CSC Managed Security Services

      CSC's Managed Security Services Tiers encompass bundled sets of Cybersecurity offerings that provide our clients with an evolutionary path to their optimal security state – the posture that is most appropriate for them given their particular business and risk profile. CSC's Managed Security Services convey a dynamic capability such that our clients continually adapt as their business and associated risks change – delivering gains in operational efficiency and effectiveness while enhancing situational awareness and ability to adapt.

      CSC Global Cybersecurity — provides full scope Incident Response (IR) services, both on a retained service and emergency response, to commercial and government clients around the world. If you have an active cyber incident, our IR Emergency Hotline (numbers at left) should be your first call, as we provide immediate help in the form of highly trained technical 'GO' teams to identify and remediate the problem, a full service incident management system for secure coordination, regulatory reporting (for all states and most countries), and stakeholder communications, as well as detailed forensics, advanced malware analysis, and interfaces to business, corporate and legal departments.
