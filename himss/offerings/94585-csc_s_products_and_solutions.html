CSC's Products and Solutions

CSC's Featured Products and Solutions at HIMSS14

CSC provides complete solutions that directly address the Institute for Healthcare Improvement’s ‘Triple Aim’ – better care for individuals, better health for populations, lower per-capita costs. Our capabilities range from world-class next generation infrastructure, through expert consultancy and technology management services, to unique healthcare-specific software and business process solutions.

We share your number one priority: improving health outcomes for patients and populations.

Coordinated Care

Improve patient care by making critical information available in the right place at the right time

  • Care Management and CareTransitions
  • Health Analytics to Support Population Health
  • EHR Provision, Implementation and Management
  • Software Integration
 

Next-Generation Healthcare IT

Increase efficiency through cost-effective new approaches to infrastructure provision

  • Infrastructure-as-a Service Including Storage, Compute and Workplace
  • Cybersecurity Risk Assessment and Threat Management
  • Harnessing Big and Fast Data for Business Advantage

Enterprise Health

Strengthen system-wide effectiveness by providing tools to help health enterprises transform their operations

  • Strategic Advisory and Sourcing
  • System Implementation and Integration
  • Applications Modernization, Testing and Mobility
 

Government Health

U.S. Federal Health

As nations debate how best to serve the health of citizens, CSC is helping governments transform healthcare with Better Information for Better DecisionsSM.

U.S. State Health

Today, state governments face big challenges. It's not easy to manage federally mandated health insurance programs and deliver quality healthcare services when dealing with increasing budget constraints, impending healthcare reform and rising Medicaid costs. To effectively respond, you need the latest healthcare IT business processes and solutions.

For more information, please contact us.
